About

Oasis Bot is a feature-packed Discord bot designed to make your server more engaging and enjoyable. With a built-in economy system, users can earn coins, play exciting games like slots and blackjack, and take part in interactive social commands. Whether you're looking for fun commands, daily rewards, or just a way to keep your community active, Oasis Bot has something for everyone. Get started today by typing /help and explore all the ways Oasis Bot can enhance your server!

Features

Command List

See all the commands that the bot has

Type /help to get started!


Commands:
!user - Shows your balance
!balance - Shows your balance
!gif - Shows a random gif
!coinflip ammount - Flips a coin and gives you money or takes it away
!slots ammount- Plays a slot machine
!blackjack ammount - Plays a game of blackjack
!quotes - Shows a random quote
!all-in - Plays a game of all-in. Your chance depends on the multiplier.
!birthday m-d-y- Adds your birthday
!shows_birthday - Shows your birthday
!daily- gives you a daily reward
/steal- steal money from someone
/ship- ship someone with someone
